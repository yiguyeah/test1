Top 3 Facts of New Zealand!

Listed below are some interesting facts New Zealand holds that you may not know!

1. First Country to allow women to vote!

In 1893, New Zealand became the first country in the world to grant women the right to vote!


2. No Snakes!

Surprisingly, despite its strong connection with nature, New Zealand is one of few countries in the world without these reptiles!


3. Waitangi Day

Celebrated on February the 6th, Waitangi Day marks the day the Treaty of Waitangi was signed between British Troops and Māori people. This is one of New Zealand's most important dates and celebrated through festivals likewise.
