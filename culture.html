Top 3 Cultural Highlights of New Zealand

New Zealand is home to one of the most diverse cultures in the world, here are some key highlights!

1. Haka

The haka is a traditional Māori war dance performed on many important occasions. These occasions include festivals, schools, and most iconicly at All Blacks sporting events.


2. Matariki

Matariki marks the Māori New Year, characterised by 9 glowing stars located in the skies at night. Celebrated in June, it is enjoyed through festivals and events across the country.


3. Waitangi Day

Celebrated on February the 6th, Waitangi Day marks the day the Treaty of Waitangi was signed between British Troops and Māori people. This is one of New Zealand's most important dates and celebrated through festivals likewise.
